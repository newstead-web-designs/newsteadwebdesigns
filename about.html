About Us

Newstead Web Designs

Based in Newstead, Launceston, Tasmania, we create custom websites for Australian small businesses. Every site is hand-coded from scratch to deliver speed, security, and a seamless user experience.

Hi, I'm Sylvia, founder and lead developer. I combine creativity and logic to design websites that are both functional and visually appealing. With a background in mathematics and art, I approach each project with precision and balance, ensuring your website reflects your brand and connects with your audience.

Why Hand-Coded Matters

Templates can be convenient, but custom, hand-coded websites offer real advantages:

  • Faster Performance: Lean code means faster load times and smoother user experience.
  • Stronger Security: Minimal third-party plugins reduce vulnerabilities.
  • Tailored Design: Every element is built for your brand and audience.
  • Easier Maintenance: Clean, efficient code keeps updates simple and efficient.

Our Process

We follow a straightforward, four-step process to ensure your website meets your goals:

  1. Plan: Share your goals and content so we can design a website tailored to your brand.
  2. Develop: We hand-code your site with usability, performance, and audience in mind.
  3. Launch: Your fully tested site goes live, ready for visitors.
  4. Maintain: Web hosting with optional content updates to keep your site secure and current.
