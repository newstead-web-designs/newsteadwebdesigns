Starting a Small Business Website

Published: 5 January 2026

A small business website is often your first impression online. Building a clear, accessible, and professional site helps attract and retain customers. Proper planning ensures your website reflects your brand and works effectively from day one.

If you're unsure where to start, Newstead Web Designs specialises in supporting Australian small businesses with hand-coded websites that are professional, responsive, and easy to maintain.

Tip: Define your brand, target audience, and the purpose of your website before starting design or development.

1. Define Your Website Goals

Identify what you want your website to achieve:

  • Showcase products or services
  • Provide business information (hours, location, contact)
  • Capture leads or inquiries
  • Reflect your brand identity online

2. Choose the Right Website Platform

Select a solution that suits your needs and growth plans:

  • Custom hand-coded website – flexible, scalable, fully branded
  • Website builder – faster to launch, less technical setup
  • Reliable hosting – secure, fast, and easy to maintain

3. Focus on User Experience

A user-friendly website keeps visitors engaged and encourages action:

  • Simple, intuitive navigation
  • Readable content with clear calls-to-action
  • Responsive design for mobile and tablet users

4. Make Your Website Accessible

Ensure everyone can use your site, including people with disabilities:

  • Readable fonts and sufficient contrast
  • Descriptive alt text for images
  • Keyboard and screen reader-friendly navigation

5. Optimise for Search Engines

SEO helps potential customers find your website:

  • Descriptive headings and content
  • Fast loading and clean code
  • Structured content for users and search engines

6. Plan for Ongoing Maintenance

Keep your website secure, functional, and up-to-date:

  • Update software and plugins regularly
  • Monitor performance and fix issues
  • Refresh content and visuals to stay relevant

Useful Resources

Official Australian resources to help small business owners get started.

  • Business.gov.au – Starting Your Business
  • Australian Taxation Office – Starting a Business
  • ASIC – Starting a Company
