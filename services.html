Our Services

Web Design & Development

At Newstead Web Designs, we specialise in creating custom, hand-coded websites that combine clean design with reliable performance. Each site is built from the ground up to reflect your brand, engage your audience, and support your business goals. We focus on usability, accessibility, and lasting quality — delivering websites that not only look professional but also work seamlessly across all devices.

Why Hand-Coded Matters

Many web developers rely on page builders or pre-made templates for convenience — but at Newstead Web Designs, we hand-code every website from the ground up. This approach takes more care and precision, but the results are worth it: faster performance, stronger security, and a site that truly reflects your brand.

By writing clean, efficient HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, we remove unnecessary bulk and plugins that often slow down or weaken websites. The result is a lightweight site that loads quickly, performs reliably, and can easily adapt as your business grows — with no hidden dependencies or plugin conflicts.

The Benefits of a Hand-Coded Website

  • Faster Performance: Lean, optimised code means shorter load times and smoother navigation.
  • Better SEO: Google rewards fast, well-structured websites with higher visibility in search results.
  • Stronger Security: Fewer third-party tools mean fewer vulnerabilities — your site is harder to hack.
  • Tailored Design: Every detail is built around your brand, not restricted by a template.
  • Easier Maintenance: Clean code makes it simple to update and scale your website over time.

Hand-coded websites are faster, more reliable, and built to last. They deliver a better experience for your visitors — and better results for your business.

Web Maintenance

We also provide ongoing maintenance and support to keep your website secure, up to date, and performing at its best. From content updates and technical improvements to regular security checks and hosting management, we ensure your site continues to run smoothly long after launch. Our goal is to give you confidence and peace of mind — knowing your online presence is reliable, professional, and ready to grow with your business.

Our Process

We follow a straightforward, four-step process to ensure your website meets your goals:

  1. Plan: Share your goals and content so we can design a website tailored to your brand.
  2. Develop: We hand-code your site with usability, performance, and audience in mind.
  3. Launch: Your fully tested site goes live, ready for visitors.
  4. Maintain: Web hosting with optional content updates to keep your site secure and current.
